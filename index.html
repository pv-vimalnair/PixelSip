PixelSip Privacy Policy

Effective June 6, 2026

PixelSip does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information, browsing activity, or usage analytics.

Information stored locally

PixelSip stores only the information needed to provide its hydration-reminder functionality:

  • Current timer state and remaining reminder time
  • Quiet-hour preferences
  • Pause or reminder status

This information remains in Chrome's local extension storage on the user's device. It is not sent to the developer or any third party.

Data collection and sharing

PixelSip does not use advertising, analytics, external servers, third-party tracking, or remote code. It does not collect health information or maintain a history of water consumption.

Changes

If PixelSip's privacy practices change, this page will be updated before the changed version is published.
